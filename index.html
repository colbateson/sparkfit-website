Why most plans fail

You do not need another perfect plan. You need one you will repeat.

Long sessions, strict meal plans and all-or-nothing routines sound good on day one. Then work gets busy, the kids need something, energy drops and the plan disappears.

SparkFit is built around consistency first. Short workouts remove the time excuse. Simple macros remove the food confusion. Coach guidance keeps the focus on the next useful action.
