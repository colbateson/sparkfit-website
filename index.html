Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 27, 2025

SparkFit ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile fitness application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy.

1. Local-First Data Privacy

Unlike most fitness apps, SparkFit stores your personal health data (weight, height, age, activity logs, and nutrition data) exclusively on your mobile device. We do not maintain a central database of your personal fitness metrics.

2. Information We Use

We do not collect personal identifiers like your name or email unless you voluntarily contact us for support. The app uses the data you input locally to calculate macro targets and workout intensity through our on-device algorithms.

3. AI Interactions

Conversations with the Macro Monster AI Coach are processed to provide immediate feedback. These queries are not linked to a persistent user profile and are not sold to third-party advertisers.

4. Important: Data Loss

Because we do not store your data on our servers, SparkFit cannot recover your progress if you delete the app or lose your device. We recommend using your phone's native cloud backup systems (iCloud/Google Drive) to secure your app data.
