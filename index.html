Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

SparkFit ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information within our mobile application for iOS and Android.

1. Local Data Storage

SparkFit is built on a privacy-first architecture. All of your personal health data, including weight, height, age, workout logs, and meal plans, is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this personal health data to our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store your name, email address (unless provided via support), or precise location. We do not use trackers or third-party advertising IDs to build profiles of our users.

3. Data Security

Because your data resides on your physical device, its security is subject to your device's security settings (FaceID, Passcode, etc.). We recommend keeping your device updated to ensure the best protection.

4. Data Loss

Since we do not sync your data to a cloud server, deleting the SparkFit app or losing your device will result in the permanent loss of all your progress data. We cannot recover this data for you.
