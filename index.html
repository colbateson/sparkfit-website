Frequently Asked Questions

Is SparkFit truly free of subscriptions and ads?

Yes. SparkFit is committed to providing a distraction-free and affordable fitness journey. We have no subscriptions and you will see no advertisements, ever. The app is offered as a one-time purchase.

What kind of personalization does the app offer?

SparkFit customizes your experience based on your current weight, desired fitness goal (e.g., Build Muscle, Lose Fat), preferred workout duration, and unit preference (Metric/Imperial). This is used to calculate your daily caloric and macro targets.

What happens to my data if I get a new phone?

Important: Currently, all your progress data (workouts, streaks, weight logs) is stored directly on your specific device. If you delete the app or switch to a new phone, your data will be lost. Your license will still be valid via the App Store/Google Play, but your progress will not carry over. We are actively working to implement cloud sync as a future feature.

How is the AI Trainer useful?

The AI Trainer acts as an immediate resource for quick questions. It can provide context on nutrition, suggest modifications to a workout, or offer motivation and tips without the need to wait for a human response.

Privacy Policy for SparkFit

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

At SparkFit, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. We do not sell your personal data. We do not display advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when you register in the App, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, when you participate in activities in the App (like posting your weight), or otherwise contact us. The personal data that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the App, and may include:

  • Identity Data: Name (optional).
  • Fitness Data: Current weight, height, age, fitness goals (e.g., build muscle, lose fat).

Data Automatically Collected: The App generates data regarding your usage, such as workout completion times, streaks, and achievement badges.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive for the following purposes:

  • To personalize your experience and calculate tailored nutrition plans and workout schedules.
  • To track your progress and display statistics within the App (e.g., weight trends, workout streaks).
  • To improve our App and develop new features based on usage patterns.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Currently, all your personal and fitness data is stored locally on your device. This is by design to ensure maximum privacy and minimal external data sharing.

WARNING: Since data is stored locally, deleting the App or switching devices will result in the permanent loss of your progress data. Your license will still be valid via the App Store/Google Play, but your progress will not carry over. We are actively working to implement cloud sync as a future feature.

Security: We implement security measures designed to protect your information. However, please remember that no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services that track your activity.
