Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 27, 2025

1. Local-First Data Philosophy

SparkFit is built on a "Privacy-First" architecture. All personal health data, including weight, body metrics, workout history, and meal plans, is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

2. Information Collection

We do not require an account to use the app. We do not collect names, phone numbers, or physical addresses. If you contact support via email, we use that address only to respond to your inquiry.

3. AI Processing

Interaction with the AI Coach is processed via encrypted channels. These queries are used to generate responses and are not stored in a way that identifies your personal fitness profile.

4. App Store & Analytics

We may receive anonymized crash reports or usage statistics through Apple App Store Connect or Google Play Console to help improve app stability.

5. Data Retention

Because your data is local, deleting the SparkFit app results in the permanent deletion of your data. We cannot recover lost data for you.
